Data Privacy Policy

1. Our Privacy Commitment

At VeriFact, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our news aggregation services.

2. Data We Collect

Search & Query Data:
  • Your news search queries and topics
  • Timestamps of your news searches
  • Categories and topics you follow
  • Source preferences and interactions
Account Information:
  • Username and account credentials
  • Account creation and login timestamps
  • News reading history and preferences
  • Source verification settings
Usage Analytics:
  • Anonymous news reading patterns (no personal identifiers)
  • Service performance metrics
  • Error logs and system diagnostics
  • Source popularity statistics

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Aggregate and deliver news content based on your preferences
  • Personalize Content: Show relevant news sources and topics
  • Source Curation: Improve our verified source selection
  • Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive behavior
  • Analytics: Generate anonymous statistics to improve news aggregation

4. Data Protection Measures

We protect your data with:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Access: Strict authentication and authorization controls
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel can access your data
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • No Third Parties: We never sell or share your personal data with third parties

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • News Search History: Retained for 12 months to improve content recommendations
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Source Preferences: Retained until you change your preferences
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained indefinitely for service improvement
  • System Logs: Retained for 90 days for security purposes

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate personal information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your search history and account data
  • Opt-out: Disable certain data collection features

7. Cookies and Tracking

VeriFact uses:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login and session management
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage statistics (optional)

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored and processed within our secure infrastructure. We do not transfer personal data outside our secure environment without your explicit consent.

9. Children's Privacy

VeriFact is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Contact Us

If you have privacy questions or need to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@verifact.com
  • Response time: Within 30 days
  • We will investigate and respond to all privacy concerns

By using VeriFact, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
